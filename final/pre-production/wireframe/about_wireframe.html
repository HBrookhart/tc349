Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide friendly, helpful and knowledgable service to people of all ages by enriching their quality of life through traditional resources and technology, and to promote the joy of life-long learning.

Your Library Card

Getting a Library Card

Getting your free Iosco-Arenac District Library card couldn't be easier!

  1. Go to any of our eight branches and show your identification.
  2. Complete the registration card.
  3. Start using your shiny, new card to check out materials right away.
Children under 18 must have a parent or guardian present to register.

Renewing a Library Card

Your card will have an expiration date. Once a card is expires, you can renew it by providing valid identification.
If a card is lost, you must reapply and pay a replacement fee of $3.00.

Library Policies

How To Be A Responsible Library Patron

  1. Return materials on time.
  2. Return materials in the same condition that you checked them out. Keep books away form water, coffee,juice, sand, kids with crayons, and chewing dogs.
  3. Pay all fines and fees promptly.
  4. Don't use CDs for coatsers,Frisbees or candle snuffers.
  5. Inform the library of any changes in your address or telephone number.
  6. Do not let others use your library card.
  7. Respect time limits when using public computers at the library.
  8. The library is not a daycare. Be aware of your children and their actions while in the library.
  9. Always present your library card when checking out materials.

Fines and Fees

Borrowed material is considered overdue if it is not returned to the library on or before the due date.
Overdue materials are $.10 per day. The maximum fine per returned item is $5.00.
If a book is lost or damaged beyond repair, the borrower will be charged a fee equal to the retail price of the material at the time of check-out.
